Vision & Purpose

SPORT AS A
PATHWAY OUT

QHAKAZA – meaning "to shine" in Zulu – works toward empowering youth and emerging communities through sport-based development within Southern KwaZulu-Natal, with ambitions to expand nationally. Grassroots sport should be accessible to all — especially in the least developed places where talent is most abundant.

🏉
School Sport Programs & Leagues
We target primary schools in the community, establish league systems, and identify talent — creating structured pathways for young athletes to grow and compete.
🛡️
Safety Net Against Peer Pressure
QHAKAZA provides a structured alternative to poverty, drugs, teenage pregnancy and peer pressure — giving children a positive environment where they belong.
🤝
Skills, Mentorship & Wellness
Beyond the field, we run workshops focused on health, wellness, teamwork and life skills — equipping young people for everything life brings.
Founder & CEO · QHAKAZA
The Man Behind The Mission

GROMINGO B.
MZUNGA

"I saw too many gifted children give up — not because they lacked talent, but because no one gave them a shirt, a ball, or a reason to believe. QHAKAZA is that reason."

Gromingo B. Mzunga is the Founder and CEO of Qhakaza Youth Community Foundation. Born and raised in the very communities he now serves, Gromingo channelled his intimate understanding of hardship, peer pressure, and untapped potential into building something permanent — a foundation that refuses to let circumstances determine destiny.

Operating out of Gamalakhe in the UGU District of Southern KwaZulu-Natal, he built a registered, SARS-compliant NPO from the ground up — entirely free of charge to every family it serves. His vision is local today, and national tomorrow.
