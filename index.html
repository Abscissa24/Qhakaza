Vision & Purpose

SPORT AS A
PATHWAY OUT

QHAKAZA – meaning "to shine" in Zulu – works toward empowering youth and emerging communities through sport-based development within Southern KwaZulu-Natal, with ambitions to expand nationally. Grassroots sport should be accessible to all — especially in the least developed places where talent is most abundant.

🏉

School Sport Programs & Leagues

We target primary schools, establish league systems, and identify talent — creating structured pathways for young athletes to grow and compete.

🛡️

Safety Net Against Peer Pressure

QHAKAZA provides a structured alternative to poverty, drugs, teenage pregnancy and peer pressure — giving children a positive environment where they belong.

🤝

Skills, Mentorship & Wellness

Beyond the field, we run workshops focused on health, wellness, teamwork and life skills — equipping young people for everything life brings.

The Man Behind The Mission

GROMINGO B.
MZUNGA

Gromingo B. Mzunga is the Founder and CEO of Qhakaza Community Youth Foundation, a registered non-profit organisation based in Gamalakhe, operating in Southern KwaZulu-Natal under the UGU District Municipality.

Under his leadership, QHAKAZA works to ensure that sport is accessible to all young people in rural and township communities — identifying talent, developing coaches, and creating league systems among local schools, entirely free of charge to the families it serves.
